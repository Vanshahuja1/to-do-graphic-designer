To Do List for Graphic Designers

Task Assign

  • Create visually appealing graphics for social media and marketing materials.
  • Design UI/UX elements for websites and mobile applications.
  • Develop branding and logo designs according to client requirements.
  • Enhance and edit images using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • Collaborate with content and marketing teams for design consistency.
  • Ensure designs are optimized for different screen sizes and resolutions.
  • Stay updated with the latest design trends and software updates.
  • Prepare print-ready files and collaborate with printing vendors.
